About

I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented software tester and data scientist with over 5 years of experience in the field. My expertise lies in testing software applications to ensure they meet quality standards, as well as leveraging data to drive business decisions and improve processes.

With a background in computer science, I have a solid understanding of programming languages, software development methodologies, and data structures. This knowledge allows me to effectively test software applications and identify potential bugs and issues. I also have experience in manual and automated testing, ensuring that software applications are thoroughly tested before release.

In my role as a data scientist, I have leveraged data to drive insights and inform decision making. I have experience in data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning. I am skilled in using tools such as Python, R, and SQL to manipulate and analyze data, and I am always looking for ways to improve data-driven processes.

In short, I am a proactive, problem-solving individual
